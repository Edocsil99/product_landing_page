About Us

We teach the way of the Edocsil Brisngr Fist, otherwise known as the Unconquerable Fire Fist. Founded during the Great Depression, we are a system that takes in people of all ages and teaches valuable self-defense and life skills. We are proud to say that many of our students have gone on to have successful careers, relationships, and happy families. This martial-arts system takes inspiration from many different styles, such as Shaolin Kung Fu, Bagua, Aikido, and Krav Maga. The movements range from fluid to hard-striking. In our system, we instill consistency, commitment, and perseverance.
